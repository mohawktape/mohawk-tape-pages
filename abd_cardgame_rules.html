Game Pack Instructions

Welcome to your game pack! Inside, you’ll find several envelopes, each labeled with a specific activity on the front.

Every envelope contains three visible Option Cards. Read through all three and choose one Option Card to customize your activity to your liking.

You’ll also find a few Mystery Cards inside each envelope. Each Mystery Card adds a unique twist to your chosen activity. Select only one Mystery Card — it will guide your experience toward either a spicy or connective mood.

Each Mystery Card is marked with chili peppers 🌶️ (for spice) and chain links 🔗 (for connection). The more chili peppers, the hotter your activity will be! Remember: only read your Mystery Card after you’ve selected it.

Caution: Three-chili cards are intensely spicy and meant only for those feeling bold and adventurous.

You’ll also receive three Super Cards:

Skip – Skip this Mystery Card entirely.

Reroll – Draw a new Mystery Card to replace the one you picked.

Swap – Draw a second Mystery Card, then choose between the two.
